About The Company

Petsi is a pet management platform. It allows you to continuously monitor your animals and provides you with effective and revolutionary tools to better manage your animals' needs. Thus, this platform makes it possible to create a network that brings together owners and veterinarians in order to facilitate exchanges and knowledge sharing. For veterinarians, Petsi provides digital patient management and instant communication with colleagues. In addition, Petsi offers a dedicated space for any animal exchange action with their data: sale, adoption.
